Hi there! My name is Serhat and welcome to my learning journal

After several months of learning, I finally decided to start my journey as a frontend developer.I am excited to learn more and share my knowledge with others. I hope you enjoy my blog posts and find them helpful.

How I stay committed to learning

I like to think of myself as as lifelong learner. I used to spend hours and hours learning, then try to create simple projects using what I learned or work new techniques into existing projects.

While that was fun, I felt like it would be helpful to share what I was learning and most things about my journmey with the world.

How I got started

I started simple gradually grew my learning journal site. I would take ntoes about what I was learning. After each learning session, I'd use my notes to not only reflect on what I learned but also write short summaries of what I learned using my own words.

That helped me grok what I was learning, and I realized that posting my learning summaries was also helping others and stay motivated.
